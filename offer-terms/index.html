Offer Terms

Effective from: January 2026

Section 01

The Offer

This Offer provides subscription benefits at a discounted price, for the period specified in the Offer. The subscription will start upon confirmation of the Offer on your Subscriptions page in the Google Play Store.

Section 02

Duration

After you redeem the Offer, you can confirm the Offer Period end date on your Subscriptions page in the Google Play Store. The Offer is available for a limited time.

Section 03

Requirements & Eligibility

The Offer requires a Google Payments account with a qualifying form of payment at sign-up. This Offer is limited to new users and existing members and may not be combined with other offers.

Section 04

Recurring Billing & Cancellation

Important Notice

On the date your Offer Period ends, your subscription will continue and your payment method will be automatically charged the standard subscription price — until you cancel.

You can cancel at any time. No refunds are available for partial billing periods. To avoid being charged, cancel before the end of the Offer Period. If you cancel during the Offer Period, your membership remains active until the Offer Period ends.

Section 05

Payment & Renewal Details

Payment is charged to your Google Account upon purchase confirmation. The subscription automatically renews at the end of each period unless you turn it off at least 24 hours before the current period ends via Google Play Settings.

Secure Processing

All payments are processed securely via Google Play Billing. We never store your credit card or banking information.

Section 06

Manage Your Subscription

To cancel or manage your subscription and disable auto-renewal:

1
Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
2
Sign in to the correct Google Account linked to your subscription.
3
Open the Menu and tap "Payments & subscriptions."
4
Tap Subscriptions.
5
Select the subscription you want to cancel.
6
Tap Cancel subscription and follow the prompts.
Please Note

Uninstalling the app does not cancel your subscription. You must cancel through Google Play to stop future charges.
